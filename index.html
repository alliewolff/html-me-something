The Life & Times of Milo Wolff

Milo Wolff came to us in May of 2015 at 8 weeks old. He was the runt of the litter and weighed only 3 pounds when we got him.

Milo immediately made himself at home and stole our hearts.

Since then, he has celebrated two birthdays, our engagement and our wedding.

My life wouldn't be the same without our Milo pup, and it certainly wouldn't be as fun.
